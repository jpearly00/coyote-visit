The manifesto

Most mercantiles focus on: stocking shelves and moving volume.

We focus on

the crossroads.

Handpicked provisions, honest sourcing, a place where locals and travelers meet the desert on equal footing.

How we operate

A mercantile runs on three motions.

Step 001

Source.

Walk the farmers' markets. Call the makers. Taste everything. If a dirt road leads to a better pantry, drive it.

Step 002

Curate.

Only what earns a place on the shelf. Nothing makes the cut because a rep pushed it. Quality wins, always.

Step 003

Serve.

Open doors at six. Brew coffee. Pack lunches. Send you out to the desert with what you need — nothing you don't.
